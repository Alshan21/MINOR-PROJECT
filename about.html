About Us

Welcome to PosturePro! Our mission is to enhance your workout efficiency and safety by ensuring you maintain correct posture during exercises.

Our Model

Our deep learning model leverages advanced computer vision techniques to monitor and analyze your body posture in real-time while you exercise. Using your device's camera, the model detects your movements and provides instant feedback, helping you correct your form on the spot.

How It Works

Here's how the process works:

  • Log in to your account and select the exercise (push-ups or squats) you want to perform.
  • Allow the model to access your camera.
  • The model will start analyzing your posture in real-time and provide immediate feedback to ensure you're performing the exercise correctly.

Benefits of Using PosturePro

  • Injury Prevention: Correct posture reduces the risk of strains and injuries during workouts.
  • Improved Efficiency: Proper form maximizes the effectiveness of your exercises, helping you achieve better results.
  • Real-Time Feedback: Instant feedback allows you to make immediate adjustments, ensuring your workouts are always on point.
  • Personalized Experience: Our model adapts to your specific movements, offering tailored advice to improve your form.
  • Convenience: You can use PosturePro anytime, anywhere, making it a versatile tool for your fitness journey.
